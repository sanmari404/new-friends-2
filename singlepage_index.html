1. Introduction

 

    “Hello, my name is Marie.
     What’s your name?”

     

 

      “My name is Sunny.
       Nice to meet you”

       

 

“Nice to meet you too.”


2. Personal Information

 

     “Where do you come from Sunny?”

            

 

"I come from Korea. And you?”


 

“I come from Congo.”


3. Personal Information

 

“How long have you been in New Zealand?”



 

“Six months. And you?”




 

“I have been here for two years.”





4. Personal Information

 

“Where do you live now Sunny?”




 

“I live in Hamilton. My address is 60 Howden Road, Fairfield. How about you?”


 

“I live in Hamilton too. My address is 143 Wellington Street, Hamilton East.”
